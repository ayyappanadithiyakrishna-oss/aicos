The Process

Three rounds. No shortcuts.

1

Analyze

Each agent independently evaluates the ticker against their mandate. Victoria builds the bear case. Maximilian builds the bull case. Sage looks at what everyone else will miss. No agent sees another's work.

2

Deliberate

Every agent reads every other agent's analysis and responds. Devlin stress-tests the majority view. Aria reconciles short-term disagreements with her decade-long thesis. Convictions shift, strengthen, or break.

3

Vote

Final signals are cast with conviction weights. The committee's decision is the conviction-weighted consensus: buy, sell, hold, reduce, or avoid. If conviction clears the threshold, the position is sized and the order goes to Alpaca.
